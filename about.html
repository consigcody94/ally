About Alliance Operations

Protecting what matters most in Florida for over 15 years

Veteran-Owned Elite Private Security

Alliance Operations is a veteran-owned and operated private security firm that has been at the forefront of tactical security excellence in Florida for over 15 years. Founded and led by military veterans, we bring combat-tested expertise and military discipline to every protection assignment.

Our veteran leadership ensures that every team member embodies the core values of service, precision, and unwavering commitment to mission success. We specialize in elite private security services including executive protection, VIP security, corporate asset protection, and high-value event security.

From uniformed guards to plainclothes executive protection, vehicle patrols to comprehensive security consulting, we deliver peace of mind through vigilant, professional, and discreet security services backed by military-grade training and tactical excellence.
